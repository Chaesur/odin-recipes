Cereal

Description:

The perfect bowl of cereal involves much more nuance than simply pouring milk and cereal into a bowl.

Ingredients:

  • Your favorite bowl and spoon
  • Your preferred cereal
  • Freshly squeezed milk

Steps:

  1. Approach your cow, ask them if they are ready to be milked. Once they are ready, proceed to step 2.
  2. Tickle the udder until it blasts white liquid gold into thine bowl
  3. Your bowl now full of liquid, walk it carefuly to your chef station, so you do not spill a drop
  4. Normally, any sane person would pour their cereal before the milk, but this is a special case
  5. Pour your cereal with the gentle grace of a figure skater gliding across a rink of ice
  6. Turn on cartoons to enhance the cereal atmosphere
  7. Eat & Enjoy!
