Leftover Pizza

Description:

Sometimes pizza is even better the second day, after it's been sitting in your fridge for a lil' bit.

Ingredients:

  • Yesterday's leftover pizza
  • A microwave
  • Some ranch if you like that type of thing on your pizza

Steps:

  1. Order a large pizza. Eat as much as you can by yourself. If you manage to eat the whole thing, order another, we need leftovers for this recipe
  2. Wait a day
  3. Take your leftove pizza out of the fridge and put a slice or two into the microwave
  4. Heat it up in the microwave for about a minute (give or take 15 seconds depending on how strong your microwave is)
  5. Let it cool off if it's too hot. Don't wanna burn the roof of your mouth.
  6. Enjoy!
