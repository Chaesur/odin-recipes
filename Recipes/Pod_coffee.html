Pod Coffee

Description:

A nice, quick, and convenient cup of coffee.

Ingredients:

  • A Nespresso Coffee Maker
  • Coffe pods of your preferred flavor
  • A mug
  • Water

Steps:

  1. Fill up your Nespresso machine's water tank so it is full
  2. Insert your coffee pod
  3. Place your mug on the output platform
  4. Hit either the espresso or full cup button
  5. Listen to the hum of the machine fill your cup
  6. Let the vapor of steaming coffee fill your nostrils
  7. Sip it while it's hot!
